üß† How V7 Predicts the Market

V7 analyzes the market using 6 mathematical theories + AI:

  • Shannon Entropy: Measures market randomness (low = predictable, high = chaotic)
  • Hurst Exponent: Detects trends (>0.5 = trending market, <0.5 = mean-reverting)
  • Market Regime: Identifies if market is bullish, bearish, or consolidating
  • Kalman Filter: Removes noise to find true price direction
  • Bayesian Learning: Learns from past trades to improve accuracy
  • Monte Carlo Risk: Simulates 10,000 scenarios to calculate risk/reward

Signal Meanings:

  • BUY: Market likely going UP - Entry price shows where to buy, Take Profit shows target
  • SELL: Market likely going DOWN - Entry price shows where to sell, Take Profit shows target
  • HOLD: Market too uncertain - Wait for better conditions

Confidence Levels:

  • 70%+: High confidence - Strong signal with clear mathematical backing
  • 60-70%: Medium confidence - Decent signal, manageable risk
  • <60%: Low confidence - Market uncertain, usually HOLD recommended

Price Targets Explained:

  • Entry Price: The price where you should enter the trade
  • Stop Loss: Exit if price goes against you (limits losses)
  • Take Profit: Exit if price reaches target (locks in profits)
  • Risk/Reward (R:R): 1:2 means risk $1 to potentially make $2
